The missing layer

The app can show what happened. Human conversation learns why.

01 Who needs a conversation?

New residents, quiet residents, hosts-in-waiting, people who attend once, people who should meet.

02 What should we ask?

What made you feel at home? Who did you connect with? What would make it easier to come back?

03 What did we learn?

Trusted people, preferred spaces, event energy, comfort level, contribution potential, disconnection signals.

04 What happens next?

Invite, introduce, host, follow up, adjust the format, or build the next experience around real demand.
