Product workflow

From one conversation to one better next action.

A human conversation makes someone feel known while revealing what the community needs to coordinate next.

01 Find

Identify the person or segment most likely to benefit from a real conversation.

02 Guide

Give the right person the right context and one question that opens the signal.

03 Remember

Save only the useful signal the community is allowed to remember.

04 Recommend

Suggest the next person, room, invitation, host handoff, or follow-up.

05 Learn

Measure whether felt connection improved and sharpen the next recommendation.
